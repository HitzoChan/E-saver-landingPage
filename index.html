About the Project

Why E-Saver?

E-Saver was developed to address increasing electricity consumption caused by limited user awareness. Most households rely solely on monthly electric bills, which do not provide appliance-level insights.

By offering electricity tracking, cost estimation, budget monitoring, and clear visualization, E-Saver empowers households, students, schools, and small businesses to make informed energy decisions.
